Research Overview

What This Section Does

The Research section is where opportunity discovery becomes structured analysis. It surfaces ranked candidates, market context, and decision-support information designed to help users understand which names deserve deeper review.

Why It Matters

In active markets, attention is expensive. Research matters because not every symbol deserves the same amount of focus. This section helps users spend time on the setups that are showing stronger alignment, cleaner structure, and better contextual support.

Instead of scanning blindly, users can move through a filtered research process built to highlight stronger opportunities first.

How To Use It

Start by reviewing top-ranked candidates and the broader context around them. Use Research to decide which names should move from watchlist status into active review. This section works best before execution, when the goal is to narrow the field and focus on the cleanest opportunities.

Think of it as a pre-trade intelligence layer rather than a simple idea feed.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Top candidates and ranked opportunities
  • Market context summaries
  • Protected research insights
  • Knowledge pages explaining how to interpret the platform
  • Premium analysis paths for deeper review

What Makes It Different

The Research section is built around decision quality, not information overload. It is designed to show what matters most without exposing the internal formulas, ranking methods, or protected weighting logic that drive candidate selection.
