How SimpleeMrkTrade Works

SimpleeMrkTrade is a rules-based market scanning and trade-ranking platform designed to identify higher-quality opportunities and manage risk through layered decision logic.

The platform reviews multiple dimensions of market behavior, including trend conditions, momentum, liquidity, market context, and account-aware risk controls.

It does not rely on a single indicator. Instead, it combines several categories of market evidence to prioritize stronger opportunities.

Specific internal scoring formulas, weighting logic, and ranking methods are proprietary.
