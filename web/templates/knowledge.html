Account Value

Account Value is the platform’s estimate of total account worth at the moment you are viewing it.

This matters because it is the clearest top-level health signal for the account.

Cash

Cash is the amount of capital not currently tied up in active positions.

Buying Power

Buying Power is the amount of capital the system can still deploy after applying internal account limits.

Open Positions

Open Positions are trades that are still active and have not yet been closed by an exit condition.

Equity Curve

The Equity Curve is the visual history of account progression over time.

Market Regime

Market Regime describes the broader condition the market is in, such as trending, bearish, or defensive behavior.

Volatility

Volatility refers to how aggressively price is moving. Higher volatility can increase both opportunity and risk.

Market Mode

Market Mode is the system’s practical operating stance after combining regime, breadth, and internal conditions.

Use the full explanation page here: Modes Guide

Signals

Signals are possible trade opportunities identified after passing through multiple layers of analysis.

Score

Score is a relative strength measure for a setup compared to other opportunities in the same environment.

Confidence

Confidence reflects how well a setup matches the current environment. It is a measure of alignment, not certainty.

Top Candidates

Top Candidates are the opportunities the system believes deserve deeper attention after ranking and filtering.

Proof Engine

The Proof Engine is the reporting layer that tracks outcomes such as trade count, win rate, drawdown, and realized performance.

PnL

PnL means profit and loss. Unrealized PnL reflects open positions. Realized PnL reflects closed positions.

Premium Analysis

Premium Analysis provides deeper context around selected setups without exposing the platform’s full proprietary logic.

Risk Governor

The Risk Governor is a system-level protection layer that helps control exposure when conditions become unfavorable.

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. SimpleeMrkTrade is a market intelligence and decision-support platform.

How are signals generated?

Signals are generated through a multi-layer evaluation process using proprietary ranking and filtering logic.

Why do strong setups sometimes get skipped?

Because account limits, buying power, market mode, and risk protections can block otherwise attractive opportunities.

Why do modes matter so much?

Because the same setup can deserve a different response depending on whether the platform is operating normally, defensively, or under elevated stress.
