Knowledge Center

What This Section Does

The Knowledge Center explains how to interpret the platform’s outputs. It is designed to help users understand what they are seeing across dashboards, positions, signals, analytics, and premium research without needing access to the underlying proprietary engine.

Why It Matters

A strong platform is not just about generating information. It also needs to teach users how to read the information correctly. This section matters because it reduces confusion, improves decision quality, and creates a more consistent user experience.

How To Use It

Use the Knowledge Center whenever you need more context on a page, metric, or workflow. It is especially useful for understanding how to move from signal discovery to trade review, how to read position health, and how to interpret platform-level performance.

What It Covers

  • How to read signals
  • How to interpret position monitoring
  • What the analytics mean
  • How to think about ranked candidates
  • How premium analysis should be used

Important Note

Educational explanations are provided here for usability and interpretation. Internal methods, scoring relationships, filters, and engine-specific weighting logic remain proprietary.
