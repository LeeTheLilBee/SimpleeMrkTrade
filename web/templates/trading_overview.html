Trading Overview

What This Section Does

The Trading section is where live opportunities, open exposures, and execution-aware decision support come together. It is designed to help users understand what the system is seeing right now, what is currently active, and where the strongest trade setups are beginning to form.

Why It Matters

Most traders lose consistency because they react to isolated signals without enough context. The Trading section is important because it organizes market opportunities into a usable workflow. Instead of guessing what deserves attention, users can review active setups, open positions, and signal quality in one place.

This reduces noise, improves decision discipline, and helps separate random movement from actionable opportunity.

How To Use It

Start with current signals and position status. Look for alignment between active opportunities and the broader market environment. If multiple signals appear, focus first on the ones that rank well, show stronger confidence, and fit the current account and risk profile.

This section is best used as a prioritization layer. It helps users decide which setups deserve attention first, which positions need monitoring, and which trades may no longer justify capital.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Live signal flow
  • Open position monitoring
  • Position health and exit proximity
  • Trade activity and execution tracking
  • Decision-ready market opportunities

What Makes It Different

This section does not rely on one indicator or one trigger. It reflects a layered decision process that evaluates opportunity quality, current conditions, and account-aware constraints before surfacing what matters most. Specific internal ranking logic remains proprietary.
