Equity Curve

What This Page Shows

The equity curve is the visual progression of account value over time. It helps you understand whether the system is behaving consistently, whether performance is stable or unstable, and whether growth is happening smoothly or with large swings.

This page matters because single trades can be misleading. The equity curve shows the bigger picture.

How To Read It

A smoother upward curve usually suggests more controlled behavior. Sharp drops or jagged movement can suggest drawdown pressure, unstable performance, or uneven trade outcomes.

Use this page to judge system behavior over time, not to react to one individual signal.
