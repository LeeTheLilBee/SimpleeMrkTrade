Premium Hub

What This Section Does

Premium is the deeper intelligence layer of the platform. It gives qualified users access to curated analysis, richer setup summaries, chart-backed reviews, and higher-context interpretation of why certain opportunities matter.

Why It Matters

Public signal lists can show what the platform sees. Premium is where users begin to understand how to work with that information more effectively. This section matters because it adds context, not just more data.

It helps users think through stronger opportunities with more structure while protecting the proprietary internals that make the platform unique.

How To Use It

Use Premium after reviewing the broader dashboard and research views. Start with the highest-priority setups, then move into premium analysis to understand the most important supporting details, scenario framing, and chart context.

Premium is best used to improve judgment, not replace it.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Chart-backed premium analysis
  • Priority setup summaries
  • Protected reasoning snippets
  • Tier-based access to deeper insights
  • Higher-context review of selected opportunities

Protected By Design

Premium is intentionally built to reveal the most useful decision-support information without exposing the system’s full internal architecture, scoring formulas, or proprietary ranking mechanics.
